ABOUT

The National Conference on Technology for the Society (NCTS’23) provides a platform for professionals, researchers, and experts to explore into the societal impact of technology. NCTS’23 is a multi-disciplinary forum for engineers, policymakers, entrepreneurs, philosophers, researchers, social scientists, and technologists to collaborate, exchange experiences, and discuss the social implications of technology. NCTS’23 aims to bring together contributions from a variety of perspectives, disciplines, and communities for the advancement of knowledge regarding Technology and Analytics for Global Development. Participation is invited from academics and practitioners who are engaged in current research about the role and significance of technology and analytics, and who are interested in topics related to sustainability, ethics, equity, and social values for global development.
